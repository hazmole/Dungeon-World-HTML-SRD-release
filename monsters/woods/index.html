幽暗森林

殺手藤 (Assassin Vine)單獨, 隱匿, 不定型

荊棘 (d10 傷害, 1 穿透), 15 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠, 毀亂

特性: 植物

在動物的世界中，狩獵者與獵物之間存在著明顯的區別。你只需一眼就能看穿－－透過那鋒利尖牙和發光眼瞳、或利爪、或毒刺－－這個世界上的某些生物就是注定要殺戮，而某些則是注定被獵殺。或許你能發現，這類差異也同樣將這個世界上的綠葉鮮花分成了兩半。在森林結社的德魯伊們清楚知曉這件事。遊俠們，也可能會在為時已晚前發現這種植物。然而，那些平民百姓，當他們徘徊在他們不應前往之處－－當他們深入爬藤蔓生的森林深處，忽然，啪噠一聲，這些飢餓的藤索緊緊纏勒，將這些肥美的獵物拖進了灌木叢中。注意你的腳步，旅人唷。 本能: 成長茁壯

  • 長出新芽
  • 攻擊粗心大意的目標

閃現犬 (Blink Dog)集團, 小型, 魔法, 組織

啃咬 (d8 傷害), 6 HP, 4 護甲

近身

特性: 幻象

你剛才看見牠，現在就不見了。這些獵犬曾經被一名巫師領主擁有，並被灌注了某種幻術魔法構成的偽裝。牠們逃進了他領地周遭的森林，並與那做森林內的狼群和野犬繁衍後代。如果你夠幸運，你也許能透過牠們那閃閃發亮的銀色毛皮以及那怪異的嚎叫聲來找到牠們。牠們擁有一種非凡天賦，讓牠們可以不完全處在牠們看起來所在的位置，並利用這種天賦來擊倒那些遠比牠們強大的獵物。如果你發現自己必須面對一群閃現犬，不妨閉上眼睛與牠們交戰。在不被自然視覺所背叛的情況下，戰鬥可能會更輕鬆一些。正是因為這種邪佞巫術，這個世界的自然環境正逐漸被不自然的產物給玷汙。 本能: 狩獵

  • 讓自己看起來像是位於牠們不存在的位置假象
  • 召喚犬群
  • 以驚人的速度移動

半人馬 (Centaur)成群, 大型, 組織, 聰穎

弓 (d6+2 傷害, 1 穿透), 11 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠, 短程

特性: 半人半馬

「這將是這個世代前所未見的氏族集會。呼叫暴風蹄與輝矛氏族，召集白鬃與鐵脅部落，吹響號角，然後開始我們的會議－－我們要把話說出口，將我們的人民團結合一。這些人類為造船而砍伐我們古樹的歷史已經太久太久。精靈們軟弱、膽小、甘願成為那些爛泥敗類的朋友。我們將化作來自至暗森林中的淨化之火。揚起鮮紅色的戰爭旗幟！今天我們要反擊，對抗這些猿猴，並奪回屬於我們的東西！」 本能: 狂怒

  • 蹂躪敵人
  • 射出完美的正中紅心
  • 以毫不鬆懈的速度移動

混沌泥怪 (Chaos Ooze) 單獨, 異位面, 駭人, 不定型

扭曲之觸 (d10 傷害, 無視護甲), 23 HP, 1 護甲

近身

特性: 泥怪, 體內埋有其他位面的碎片

地城世界和元素位面之間的屏障，嗯，並不如你所希望的那般堅如磐石。它實際上更加鬆散。在文明不常踏足的場所經常會有，怎麼說，這種滲漏的現象。就像是稍有鬆動的大壩，零碎的混沌從隙縫中滲出。有時候，它們會像是煎盤上的蛋一樣凝固－－這就是我們為許多公會的魔法飾品提供原料的地方。很實用，對吧？不過有時候，它們會開始蠕動、擠壓，並維持這種狀態，將所有它所接觸的事物都扭曲成另一種怪異的型態。混亂招致混亂，並持續茁壯。 本能: 改變

  • 造成外觀或實質上的變化
  • 短暫地架起位面之間的橋樑

雞蛇 (Cockatrice)集團, 小型, 囤財者

喙啄 (d8 傷害), 6 HP, 1 護甲

近身

特性: 石化之觸

「我從來沒見過這種事，先生。羅德里克以為牠是隻雞，大概。可憐的羅德里克。我猜想牠應該是某種蜥蜴，不過他是對的－－牠確實有像雞一樣的鳥喙跟一身灰色羽毛。好吧，嗯，你看，我們是在森林發現牠的，就在樹下的一個巢窩裡，先生，那時我們帶著母豬外出正在尋找蘑菇。我跟羅德里克說－－是，先生，對，好，回到那隻鳥－－你知道，牠狠狠瞪著羅德里克，而他則拿著一根棍子嘗試嚇走牠好偷走那些蛋，但那傢伙啄了他的頭。牠的速度可快了。我試著把他拉開，但他變得越來越慢……是，就像你現在看到的那樣，先生。就跟我們在兩年前的冬天把狗放在外面過夜一樣，全身都僵住了。可憐，愚蠢的羅德里克……牠其實不是雞也不是蜥蜴，是嗎，先生？」 本能: 守衛鳥巢

  • 讓某人開始緩慢地轉變成石頭

樹精 (Dryad)單獨, 魔法, 聰穎, 狡猾

壓制藤鞭 (低[2d8]傷害), 12 HP, 2 護甲

近身

特性: 植物

More beautiful by far than any man or woman born in the civil realms. To gaze upon one is to fall in love. Deep and punishing, too. Thing is, they don’t love—not the fleshy folk who often find them, anyway. Their love is a primal thing, married to the woods—to a great oak that serves as home and mother and sacred place to them. It’s a curse to see one, too, they’ll never love you back. No matter what you do. No matter how you pledge yourself to them, they’ll always spurn you. If ever their oak comes to harm, you’ve not only the dryad’s wrath to contend with, but in every nearby village there’s a score of men with a secret longing in their heart, ready to murder you where you sleep for just a smile from such a creature. 本能: 熱愛自然

  • 引誘一個凡人
  • 融身入樹
  • 讓大自然與他們為敵

鷹王 (Eagle Lord)集團, 大型, 組織, 聰穎

禽爪 (高[2d8]+1 傷害, 1 穿透), 10 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 強健飛翼

Some the size of horses. Bigger, even—the kings and queens of the eagles. Their cry pierces the mountain sky and woe to those who fall under the shadow of their mighty wings. The ancient wizards forged a pact with them in the primordial days. Men would take the plains and valleys and leave the mountaintops to the eagle lords. These sacred pacts should be honored, lest they set their talons into you. Lucky are the elves, for the makers of their treaties yet live and when danger comes to elvish lands, the eagle lords often serve as spies and mounts for them. Long-lived and proud, some might be willing to trade their ancient secrets for the right price, too. 本能: 掌管高空

  • 從天空襲擊
  • 將某人抓到空中
  • 應古老盟約的呼召而來

精靈戰士 (Elvish Warrior)成群, 聰穎, 組織

利劍 (高[2d6] 傷害), 3 HP, 2 護甲

近身

特性: 敏銳感官

”As with all things they undertake, the elves approach war as an art. I saw them fight, once. The Battle of Astrid’s Veil. Yes, I am that old, boy, now hush. A warrior-maiden, she was clad in plate that shone like the winter sky. White hair streaming and a pennant of ocean blue tied to her spear. She seemed to glide between the trees the way an angel might, striking out and bathing her blade in blood that steamed in the cold air. I never felt so small before. I trained with the master-at-arms of Battlemoore, you know. I’ve held a sword longer than you’ve been alive, boy, and in that one moment I knew that my skill meant nothing. Thank the gods the elves were with us then. A more beautiful and terrible thing I have never seen.” 本能: 追尋完美

  • Strike at a weak point
  • Set ancient plans in motion
  • Use the woods to advantage

精靈高等秘術師 (Elvish High Arcanist) 單獨, 魔法, 聰穎, 組織

秘法之火 (d10 傷害, 無視護甲), 12 HP, 0 護甲

短程, 遠程

特性: 敏銳感官

True elvish magic isn’t like the spells of men. Mannish wizardry is all rotes and formulas. They cheat to find the arcane secrets that resound all around them. They are deaf to the arcane symphony that sings in the woods. Elvish magic requires a fine ear to hear that symphony and the voice with which to sing. To harmonize with what is already resounding. Men bind the forces of magic to their will; Elves simply pluck the strings and hum along. The High Arcanists, in a way, have become more and less than any elf. The beat of their blood is the throbbing of all magic in this world. 本能: To unleash power

  • Work the magic that nature demands
  • Cast forth the elements

獅鷲獸 (Griffin) 集團, 大型, 組織

禽爪 (d8+3 傷害), 10 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠, 強力

特性: Wings

On first glance, one might mistake the griffin for another magical mistake like the manticore or the chimera. It looks the part, doesn’t it? These creatures have the regal haughtiness of a lion and the arrogant bearing of an eagle, but they temper those with the unshakeable loyalty of both. To earn the friendship of a griffin is to have an ally all your living days. Truly a gift, that. If you’re ever lucky enough to meet one be respectful and deferential above all else. It may not seem it but they can perceive the subtlest slights and will answer them with a sharp beak and talons. 本能: To serve allies

  • Judge someone’s worthiness
  • Carry an ally aloft
  • Strike from above

山丘巨人 (Hill Giant) 集團, 巨型, 聰穎, 組織

投擲巨岩 (d8+3 傷害), 10 HP, 1 護甲

及遠, 短程, 遠程, 強力

有看過食人魔嗎？他們比那更大，但也更笨，更壞。希望你喜歡被牛往臉上砸的感覺。 本能: 破壞一切

  • 把某物投擲出去
  • 做一件傻事
  • 震動大地

食人魔 (Ogre) 集團, 大型, 聰穎

棍棒 (d8+5 傷害), 10 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠, 強力

A tale, then. Somewhere in the not-so-long history of the mannish race there was a divide. In days when men were merely dwellers-in-the-mud with no magic to call their own, they split in two: one camp left their caves and the dark forests and built the first city to honor the gods. The others, a wild and savage lot, retreated into darkness. They grew, there. In the deep woods a grim loathing for their softer kin gave them strength. They found dark gods of their own, there in the woods and hills. Ages passed and they bred tall and strong and full of hate. We have forged steel and they match it with their savagery. We may have forgotten our common roots, but somewhere, deep down, the ogres remember. 本能: To return the world to darker days

  • Destroy something
  • Fly into a rage
  • Take something by force

鋒刃野豬 (Razor Boar) 單獨

獠牙 (d10 傷害, 3 穿透), 16 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 毀亂

鋒刃野豬的獠牙能把金屬板甲如同紙巾一樣輕易撕開。貪婪、野蠻、且勢不可當，牠們徹底凌駕於牠們那平凡的同族之上。宰一頭？確實沒有獵物比牠更能彰顯勇氣與技巧，不過我聽說以前醉漢王只挨了鋒刃野豬的一記衝撞就魂歸西天。難道你覺得自己比他更會打獵？ 本能: 撕毀

  • 將敵人撕成碎片
  • 撕毀防具和武器

薩特羊人 (Satyr) 集團, 狡猾, 魔法, 囤財者

衝鋒 (低[2d8] 傷害), 10 HP, 1 護甲

近身

特性: Enchantment

One of only a very few creatures to be found in the old woods that don’t outright want to maim, kill, or eat us. They dwell in glades pierced by the sun, and dance on their funny goat-legs to enchanting music played on pipes made of bone and silver. They smile easily and, so long as you please them with jokes and sport, will treat our kind with friendliness. They’ve a mean streak, though, so if you cross them, make haste elsewhere; very few things hold a grudge like the stubborn satyr. 本能: To enjoy

  • Pull others into revelry through magic
  • Force gifts upon them
  • Play jokes with illusions and tricks

狼人 (Werewolf) 單獨, 聰穎

啃咬 (d10+2 傷害, 1 穿透), 12 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 毀亂

特性: Weak to silver

“Beautiful, isn’t it? The moon, I mean. She’s watching us, you know? Her pretty silver eyes watch us while we sleep. Mad, too—like all the most beautiful ones. If she were a woman, I’d bend my knee and make her my wife on the spot. No, I didn’t ask you here to speak about her, though. The chains? For your safety, not mine. I’m cursed, you see. You must have suspected. The sorcerer-kings called it “lycanthropy” in their day—passed on by a bite to make more of our kind. No, I could find no cure. Please, don’t be scared. You have the arrows I gave you? Silver, yes. Ah, you begin to understand. Don’t cry, sister. You must do this for me. I cannot bear more blood on my hands. You must end this. For me.” 本能: To shed the appearance of civilization

  • Transform to pass unnoticed as beast or man
  • Strike from within
  • Hunt like man and beast

座狼 (Worg)成群, 組織

啃咬 (d6 傷害), 3 HP, 1 護甲

近身

As horses are to the civil races, so go the worg to the goblins. Mounts, fierce in battle, ridden by only the bravest and most dangerous, are found and bred in the forest primeval to serve the goblins in their wars on men. The only safe worg is a pup, separated from its mother. If you can find one of these, or make orphans of a litter with a sharp sword, you’ve got what could become a loyal protector or hunting hound in time. Train it well, mind you, for the worg are smart and never quite free of their primal urges. 本能: To serve

  • 載著騎手衝入戰場
  • 為牠的騎手帶來優勢
