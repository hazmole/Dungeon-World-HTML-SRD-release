弗米蟻工兵 (Formian Drone) 成群, 組織, 謹慎

啃咬 (d6 傷害), 7 HP, 4 護甲

近身

特性: 蟲巢聯繫, 類昆蟲

出於充分的理由，人們傳說這些生物（就跟所有昆蟲一樣）受到秩序之力所管轄。牠們的血肉身軀就是秩序的體現──一個階層分明的完美社會，每個幼蟲、若蟲、以及成蟲各司其職，清楚知曉自己在偉大蟲巢中的位置。弗米蟻族是某種古怪的物種，同時混雜了人類和螞蟻的特徵。（不過，我曾聽說在西部沙漠的帶翼部落看起來像是黃蜂，而那些在東方森林，長有巨大鋸齒前肢的部族則更像是螳螂。）高大，身披著堅硬的甲殼，以及更加頑固堅硬的心靈，這些特殊的弗米蟻族是整個蟲巢中的底層物種。牠們在山丘和蜂巢中一心一意的勞動著，享受著只有他們那異質心智能夠理解的單純喜悅。 本能: 遵循命令

  • 發出警報
  • 為蟲巢創造價值
  • 吸收

弗米蟻監工 (Formian Taskmaster) 集團, 組織, 聰穎

刺鞭 (d8 傷害), 6 HP, 3 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 蟲巢聯繫, 類昆蟲

統治帝國需要兩隻手：一手持握權杖，一手揮舞長鞭。而這些蟻族正是那根長鞭。牠們可真幸運，憑著那多出來的兩雙手臂，能揮舞的鞭子變得更多了。牠們負責監督大量成群的工兵，命令牠們建造出巨大的洞窟隧道、以及點綴著弗米蟻族棲居之地的金字塔神廟。每一百隻弗米蟻族中就有一隻，這些大塊頭比牠們蒼白、幾乎無心智的同類還要高上二到三呎，並有著更加敏欸、更加殘忍的智慧與之配合。牠們經常會無視那些身體柔軟的種族（也就是我們），只要我們不會干擾到牠們的工程就好。然而，一旦妨礙到了牠們偉大的工程計畫，那你可以預期牠們的注意力將全數降臨到你身上。相信我，你不會想要被牠們全神關注的。 本能: 發號施令

  • 命令工兵投入戰鬥
  • 使大量蟻族行動起來

弗米蟻百夫長 (Formian Centurion) 成群, 聰穎, 組織

刺矛 (高[2d6]+2 傷害), 7 HP, 3 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 蟲巢聯繫, 類昆蟲, 翅膀

無論是做為軍團的成員、弗米蟻族常備軍的士兵、或是女王身旁的禁衛軍，每個弗米蟻族的蟲巢中都大量部屬著這種最最危險的昆蟲族裔。身披更為暗沉的甲殼、且經常飾有紋路和儀式性記號，這些特徵使牠們與弗米蟻工兵區別開來。弗米蟻百夫長就是弗米蟻族的軍事力量，這可是千真萬確。出生、養育、活著的唯一目的就是殺死蟲巢裡的敵人，牠們在戰場上同心合一，百劍齊發。到目前為止，秩序的力量似乎足以讓人類免於與這些生物發生大規模的戰事，但我們確實曾在小型衝突中見過牠們的身姿──有時在酷熱中振翅，降臨於邊境城鎮、有時則從沙丘中鑽出，清洗了新開鑿的礦場。牠們的殺戮是純粹秩序的執行，毫無喜悅可言，僅僅是為了完成目標。 本能: 按照命令戰鬥

  • 齊頭並進
  • 召集援軍
  • 為蟲巢獻出生命

弗米蟻女王 (Formian Queen) 單獨, 巨型, 組織, 聰穎, 囤財者

粉碎下顎 (d10+5 傷害), 24 HP, 3 護甲

及遠, 強力

特性: 蟲巢聯繫, 類昆蟲

在每一座蟲巢的中心，無論其規模或種類，都住著一名女王。體型如巨人一般龐大，她坐在直屬禁衛軍的保護之下，被每一個工兵和監工所服侍，執行著只屬於她自己的唯一目的：產卵、哺育、散播她的族類、使蟲巢壯大繁盛。我們無法理解這些生物的心智，但我們知道她們能夠以某種方式跨越遙遠的距離與她們的孩子溝通，且在這些子嗣仍是蒼白蠕動的幼蟲時，她們就已經開始教導牠們關於泥壤、岩石、以及戰爭的種種，無須透過任何言語。殺死女王就等同是為整個蟲巢帶來無盡的混亂；一旦失去了牠們的女王，剩餘的蟻族就會在瘋狂盲目的憤怒之下彼此互相殘殺。 本能: 傳播延續弗米蟻族

  • 召集每個她所生下的弗米蟻族
  • 誕生一隻尚未成形的幼蟲突變體
  • 組織並下達命令

豺狼人追跡者 (Gnoll Tracker) 集團, 組織, 聰穎

弓 (d8 傷害), 6 HP, 1 護甲

短程, 遠程

特性: 氣味追蹤者

一旦牠們聞到你的鮮血，你就再也逃不掉了──至少在沒有諸神的干預、或公爵麾下一整隊遊騎兵的協助下是這樣。沙漠灌叢對於獨自一人的探索而言是個危險的地方，倘若你不幸摔斷了腿或吃錯了仙人掌，嗯……如果你在豺狼人發現你之前就死於飢渴，那你還能稱得上是幸運了。牠們更喜歡活生生的獵物，你瞧──對牠們來說，喀擦粉碎的骨頭以及臨死前的尖叫聲能為這頓大餐更添上一股多汁肥美的風味。令人作嘔的生物，可不是嗎？牠們會追捕你，緩慢但穩健，一直到你死去。如果你在沙漠荒風中聽見了那殘忍的吠笑，那麼你最好祈禱死神能比牠們早一步把你帶走。 本能: 捕食弱者

  • 執著地追蹤獵物
  • 趁著虛弱的時候發起攻擊

豺狼人特使 (Gnoll Emissary) 單獨, 神聖, 聰穎, 組織

儀式匕首 (d10+2 傷害), 18 HP, 1 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 敏銳嗅覺

噢，一名特使！真不錯。我猜你不知道豺狼人也有特使，對吧？沒錯，就連這些髒兮兮的鬣狗有時也得表現得友善一些。不，不不，不是對我們，也不是對那些矮人。不對。這名特使是牠們族群之中的唯一，能與牠們那滴淌血水的惡魔領主直接對談的存在。可怕嗎？說得真是太對了。每頭獵犬的背後都有著一個牽著鎖鏈的主人。這名豺狼人能聽見牠主人的聲音。牠聽見，並且服從。 本能: 分享神性的見解

  • 傳播惡魔的影響
  • 讓整個族群充滿激情

豺狼人首領 (Gnoll Alpha) 單獨, 聰穎, 組織

寶劍 (高[2d10] 傷害, 1 穿透), 12 HP, 2 護甲

近身

特性: 敏銳嗅覺

Every pack has its top dog. Bigger, maybe—that’d be the simplest way. Often, though, with these lank and filthy mutts, it’s not about size or sharp teeth but about cruelty. About a willingness to kill your brothers and eat them while the pack watches. Willingness to desecrate the pack in a way that cows them to you. If they’re that awful to each other—to their living kin—think about how they must view us. It’s hard to be mere meat in a land of carnivores. 本能: To drive the pack

  • Demand obedience
  • Send the pack to hunt

獸人鮮血勇士 (Orc Bloodwarrior) 成群, 聰穎, 組織

鋸齒刀 (d6+2 傷害, 1 穿透), 3 HP, 0 護甲

近身, 毀亂

The orcish horde is a savage, bloodthirsty, and hateful collection of tribes. There are myths and stories that tell of the origin of their rage—a demon curse, a homeland destroyed, elven magic gone wrong—but the truth has been lost to time. Every able orc, be it man or woman, child or elder, swears fealty to the warchief and their tribe and bears the jagged blade of a bloodwarrior. Men are trained to fight and kill—orcs are born to it. 本能: To fight

  • Fight with abandon
  • Revel in destruction

獸人狂戰士 (Orc Berserker) 單獨, 大型, 神聖, 聰穎, 組織

切肉刀 (d10+5 傷害), 20 HP, 0 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 突變

Stained in the unholy ritual of Anointing By The Night’s Blood, some warriors of the horde rise to a kind of twisted knighthood. They trade their sanity for this honor, stepping halfway into a world of swirling madness. This makes berserkers the greatest of their tribe, though as time passes, the chaos spreads. The rare berserker that lives more than a few years becomes horrible and twisted, growing horns or an extra arm with which to grasp the iron cleavers they favor in battle. 本能: To rage

  • Fly into a frenzy
  • Unleash chaos

獸人破壞者 (Orc Breaker) 單獨, 大型

錘子 (d10+3 傷害, 無視護甲), 16 HP, 0 護甲

近身, 及遠, 強力

“Before you set out across the hordeland, brave sir, hark a moment to the tale of Sir Regnus. Regnus was like you, sir—a paladin of the Order, all a-shine in his plated armor and with a shield as tall as a man. Proud he was of it, too—Mirrorshield, he called himself. Tale goes that he’d set his eyes on rescuing some lost priest, a kidnap from the abbey on the borders. Regnus came across some orcs in his travels, a dozen or so, and thought, as one might, that they’d be no match. Battle was joined and all was well until one of them orcs emerged from the fray with a hammer bigger than any man ought to be able to wield. Built more like an ogre or a troll, they say it was, and with a single swing, it crushed Regnus to the ground, shield and all. It were no ordinary orc, they say. It were a breaker. They can’t make plate of their own, see, so maybe it’s jealousy drives these burly things to crush and shatter the way they do. Effective tactic, though. Careful out there.” 本能: To smash

  • Destroy armor or protection
  • Lay low the mighty

獸人獨眼者 (Orc One-Eye) 集團, 神聖, 魔法, 聰穎, 組織

造成傷勢 (d8+2 傷害, 無視護甲), 6 HP, 0 護甲

近身, 及遠, 短程, 遠程

特性: 獨眼

In the name of He of Riven Sight and by the First Sacrifice of Elf-Flesh do we invoke the Old Powers. By the Second Sacrifice, I make my claim to what is mine—the dark magic of Night. In His image, I walk the path to Gor-sha-thak, the Iron Gallows! I call to the runes! I call to the clouded sky! Take this mortal organ, eat of the flesh of our enemy and give me what is mine! 本能: To hate

  • Rend flesh with divine magic
  • Take an eye
  • Make a sacrifice and grow in power

獸人薩滿 (Orc Shaman) 單獨, 聰穎, 組織

元素爆破 (d10 傷害, 無視護甲), 12 HP, 0 護甲

近身, 及遠, 短程, 遠程

特性: 元素力量

The orcs are as old a race as any. They cast bones in the dirt and called to the gods in the trees and stone as the elves built their first cities. They have waged wars, conquered kingdoms, and fallen into corruption in the aeons it took for men to crawl from their caves and dwarves to first see the light of the sun. Fitting, then, that the old ways still hold. They summon the powers of the world to work, to fight and to protect their people, as they have since the first nights. 本能: To strengthen orc-kind

  • Give protection of earth
  • Give power of fire
  • Give swiftness of water
  • Give clarity of air

獸人奴隸商人 (Orc Slaver) 成群, 隱匿, 聰穎, 組織

鞭子 (d6 傷害), 3 HP, 0 護甲

近身, 及遠

Red sails fly in the southern sea. Red sails and ships of bone, old wood and iron. The warfleet of the horde. Orcs down that way have taken to the sea, harassing island towns and stealing away with fishermen and their kin. It’s said the custom is spreading north and the orcs learn the value of free work. Taken to it like a sacred duty—especially if they can get their hands on elves. Hard to think of a grimmer fate than to live out your life on an orcish galley, back bent under the lash. 本能: To take

  • Take a captive
  • Pin someone under a net
  • Drug them

獸人陰影獵手 (Orc Shadowhunter) 單獨, 隱匿, 魔法, 聰穎

劇毒匕首 (d10 傷害, 1 穿透), 10 HP, 0 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: 陰影斗篷

Not every attack by orcs is torches and screaming and enslavement. Among those who follow He of Riven Sight, poison and murder-in-the-dark are considered sacred arts. Enter the shadowhunter. Orcs cloaked in Night’s magic who slip into camps, towns and temples and end the lives of those within. Do not be so distracted by the howling of the berserkers that you don’t notice the knife at your back. 本能: To kill in darkness

  • Poison them
  • Melt into the shadows
  • Cloak them in darkness

獸人戰酋 (Orc Warchief) 單獨, 聰穎, 組織

Iron Sword of Ages (高[2d10]+2 傷害), 16 HP, 0 護甲

近身, 及遠

特性: One-Eye blessings, Shaman blessings, Divine protection from mortal harm

There are chiefs and there are leaders of the tribes among the orcs. There are those who rise to seize power and fall under the machinations of their foes. There is but one Warchief. One orc in all the horde who stands above the rest, bearing the blessings of the One-Eyes and the Shamans both. But one who walks with the elements under Night. But one who bears the Iron Sword of Ages and carries the ancient grudge against the civil races on his shoulders. The Warchief is to be respected, to be obeyed and above all else, to be feared. All glory to the Warchief. 本能: To lead

  • Start a war
  • Make a show of power
  • Enrage the tribes

梭螺魚人間諜 (Triton Spy) 單獨, 隱匿, 聰穎, 組織

三叉戟 (低[2d10] 傷害), 12 HP, 2 護甲

近身, 短程

特性: 水棲

A fishing village caught one in their net, some time ago. Part a man and part some scaly sea creature, it spoke in a broken, spy-learned form of the common tongue before it suffocated in the open air. It told the fishermen of a coming tide, an inescapable swell of the power of some deep-sea god and that the triton empire would rise up and drag the land down into the ocean. The tale spread and now, when fishermen sail the choppy seas, they watch and worry that the dying triton’s tales were true. That there are powers deep below that watch and wait. They fear the tide is coming in. 本能: To spy on the surface world

  • Reveal their secrets
  • Strike at weakness

梭螺魚人喚潮者 (Triton Tidecaller) 集團, 神聖, 魔法, 聰穎

浪濤 (d8+2 傷害, 無視護甲), 6 HP, 2 護甲

短程, 遠程

特性: 水棲, 突變

Part priest, part outcast among their kind, the tidecaller speaks with the voice of the deeps. They can be known by their mutations—transparent skin, perhaps, or rows of teeth like a shark. Glowing eyes or fingertips, angler-lights in the darkness of their underwater kingdom. They speak in a strange tongue that can call and command creatures of the sea. They ride wild hippocampi and cast strange spells that rot through the wooden decks of ships or encrust them with barnacles heavy enough to sink. It is the tidecallers who come, now, back to the cities of the triton, bearing word that the prophecy is coming to pass. The world of men will drown in icy brine. The tidecallers speak and the lords begin to listen. 本能: To bring on The Flood

  • Cast a spell of water and destruction
  • Command beasts of the sea
  • Reveal divine proclamation

梭螺魚人潛海者 (Triton Sub-Mariner) 集團, 組織, 聰穎

魚叉 (高[2d8] 傷害), 6 HP, 3 護甲

近身, 短程, 遠程

特性: 水棲

The triton are not a militant race by nature. They shy away from battle except when the sahuagin attack, and then they only defend themselves and retreat into the depths where their foes can’t follow. This trend begins to change. As the tidecallers come to rally their people, some triton men and women take up arms. They call these generals “sub-mariners” and build for them armor of shells and hardened glass. They swim in formation, wielding pikes and harpoons and attack the crews of ships that wander too far from port. Watch for their pennants of kelp on the horizon and the conch-cry of a call to battle and keep, if you can, your boats near shore. 本能: To wage war

  • Lead tritons to battle
  • Pull them beneath the waves

梭螺魚人貴族 (Triton Noble) 集團, 組織, 聰穎

三叉戟 (d8 傷害), 6 HP, 2 護甲

近身, 短程, 遠程

特性: 水棲

傳說，早在時間之初，梭羅魚人的王室就已被揀選，由某個被世界遺忘的神祇授予了他們統管海洋中所有種族的權力。這些血脈延續至今，由父傳給女，母傳給子，將統治權代代相傳。王室中的每個成員都被允許以自己選擇的方式治理他們的城市──有些人孤身一人、或與他們的配偶一起、有些則選擇與他們的兄弟姊妹共組議會來治理國事。在過往的年代，他們以聰穎睿智和沉著穩重的血統而聞名並受人敬重，但喚潮者的預言正在改變這一切：「貴族應是強大的，而不是睿智的。」貴族們已經對此做出回應，且有人擔憂這古老的血脈將從此被永遠地改變……時間和潮汐不等人，現在回頭或許已經太晚了。 本能: 領導

  • 煽動梭羅魚人發起戰爭
  • 呼叫增援
